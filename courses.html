Applied Herbalism

In this course we want to welcome you upon the path of traditional herbal practices for healing and well-being. It is titled “Applied Herbalism” because the aim is not simply academic knowledge but instead, this course is designed to guide you from someone who has little to no knowledge of herbs and herbalism to becoming a certified Herbalist, able to apply your knowledge in your life.


By working through the lessons in each of these modules you will acquire the knowledge, skills and confidence needed to be a Certified Applied Herbalist, joining a historic tradition of health care practitioners.

Master Herbalism

This course is can be taken on its own or as a continuation from the applied herbalism course. The aim of this course is to give the student the tools they need to have confident mastery in the Herbalist tradition. Similar to the applied herbalism course, this program takes it a step further bringing in a greater range of understanding and application of herbs, herbalism and how to help others become well.


By working through the lessons and completing each assignment you will acquire the knowledge, skills and confidence needed to be a certified Master Herbalist, progressing the historic tradition of herbal health care practitioners.
