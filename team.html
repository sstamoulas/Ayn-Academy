Ustadh Wm. Halim Breiannis

is an instructor for the Islamic Learning Center in Baltimore, Md. where he lives with his wife and children. He is a light and energy worker certified in Chakra clearing. He is also a certified master and teacher of Reiki. He holds certifications in array of coaching and therapeutic techniques including hypno-therapy and working with addictions.

Ustadha Jamilah Breiannis

is a master herbalist, clinical aromatherapist and a certified doula who serves as the lead special education instructor at the Muslim Learning Center in Baltimore, Md.. She is also certified in art therapy and is a Reiki practitioner and uses all of her skills to help a wide range of issues. In this way, she has felt honored to offer assistance to children with different needs. Her heart is focused on helping others and she has gathered the skills and experience to excel.

She is a mother of 9 children, 4 of which have special needs that require some extra attention, all of which require lots of love. Many sleepless nights spent struggling but determined to never throw in the towel led her to alternative therapies and natural healing for her children and this led to her receiving her Masters degree in herbalism as well as becoming a clinical aromatherapist. Her initial aim was to help her own children feel better and succeed but, as they grew and thrived She wanted to help others and out of that came her little shop "Jamilah's Naturals".

Along with studies in herbalism, aromatherapy and other forms of alternative therapy to help people feel better from issues such as anxiety, ADHD, depression, mood disorders and attention problems, She also became a doula and is a certified C.L.S.E., helping women who struggle with infertility issues and other areas of women's health and wellness. She offers consulting, advise, and herbal products. She has written articles and booklets, been invited as a panel speaker on webinars, and is currently working on producing affordable online courses in various topics. In this way she continues to grow as a business, a service and a woman.
