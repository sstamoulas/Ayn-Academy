What's in a name?

AYN itself is actually a great name for what we seek to accomplish as the word in Arabic has several meanings, each one correlating to our aims. From the meanings is "eye" and "fountain". The eye is known as the window to the soul and this is part of what we aim to help people do - look within and do real soul searching in order to come to know themselves. We also hope to be a fountain from which beneficial and life enriching services spring forth and flow. From the meanings of this word in Arabic are also the connotations of creating, assisting, supplying and helping to rescue. Knowing this, it wasn't hard for us to choose this name for our academy.

Although, without a doubt we are people of faith, the very purpose of this academy is to be able to serve a greater audience of humanity by separating (as much as possible) our wellness modalities from any forms of proselytizing- while still always speaking from our hearts.

  • OUR MISSION: to use and share the skills we have been blessed with in order to help others.
  • OUR FOCUS: to transmit the skills we have been blessed to learn and master. At the same time however, we recognize that not everyone has the time or wherewithal to learn and so we also offer our services to those who seek them.
  • OUR METHODS: vary by situation, needs and requests of our clients. Whether interpersonal consultations in person or through online platforms, one on one or in groups we do our best to make ourselves available.

Our aim truly is to help others to heal, become whole and reach their potential.
